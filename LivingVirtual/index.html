High Speed Internet

Wireless High Speed Internet offered by LivingVirtual is the only practical alternative to cable Internet and DSL, delivering speed, reliability and accessibility where cable and DSL availability is limited. LivingVirtual has become a major source for residential and business Web access throughout the City, succeeding in closing the coverage gap left by cable and DSL providers while winning a growing number of customers who choose Living Virtual Wireless High Speed Internet over the competition.

Microwave

From 1Mbps to 1000+Mbps. All around southern Nevada livingvirtual.Net can provide you with 1st class Microwave or wireless Internet service. A city-wide fiber network has many more points that it has to traverse through to get to the final point then a microwave network. This means going through many switches or routers for data to arrive at the destination. If any of these points breaks, often the network is down. With Living Virtual Microwave this isn't a problem and we stay up.
