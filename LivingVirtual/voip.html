VoIP

The Smarter Business Solution

LivingVirtual provides enterprise-level features & benefits. Offering large-scale PBX services for any size business, monthly cost savings, low cost of purchase, flexibility in deployment, and avoidance of obsolence!

Affordability

The affordability of Hosted VoIP technology and the value business owners place upon technology is one of the reasons why this technology has been so widely adopted in the business marketplace. Many times, businesses with Hosted VoIP see their monthly outlay for phone service and phone system maintenance decrease when compared with their old traditional analog phone system. On average our clients save over 50% compared to their traditional phone bill.

Flexibility

As businesses expand they need a phone system that can grow with them and match their level of progression. With Hosted VoIP, businesses can add phones when and where they need them. Business do not have to overbuild a phone system to accommodate growth, but only add phones as they grow. Also, because they are connected to a cloud based system and not a system housed in their office, they are able to deploy phones wherever they need. If their growth necessitates phones in another location Living Virtual's Hosted VoIP phone service can accommodate their needs. To the caller, they would have no idea where the phone calls are being answered!

Call Forwarding

Call forwarding is perfect for when you need ot take office calls on you're out. It can be toggle on / off per phone.

Caller ID

With Caller ID, kyou can identify who's calling before you pick up the phone. The number and name(if available) of incoming call appears on your display.

Caller ID Blocking

LV.Net VoiceNet allows you to block your Caller ID on outgoing calls.…

Call Park

With the Call Park feature, you can "park" a callin the cloud so other employees can pick up the call. A parked call can be picked up by anyone in your organization.

Call Transfer

Now you can trasnfer calls with ease. Choose either warm transfer or cold(or blind) transfer. With warm transfer, you announce the call to the person you are transferring the call to. With a cold transfer, you send the call directly to the extension without announcing it.

Call Waiting

Call Waiting allows callers to reach you even when you are on the phone with another call.


You will hear a beep indicating an incoming call. You will then be able to put your current caller on hold and answer the new call. You can continue to alternate between parties as often as you'd like.

Conference Bridge

Host your conference calls right from your LV.Net VoiceNet phone at no extra charge.

Direct Number

On request each phone can have its own unique direct inbound dial (DID) telephone number.

Do Not Disturb

When you need quiet time to finish a project, put your telephone in a state that will not ring and calls will automatically be forwarded to voicemail(or to a busy signal if voicemail is turned off).

Extension Dialing

Call anyone in your compnay just by dialing their extension number, even if they work in differnt locations.

Flat Rate Billing

For all our standard services you pay a single rate per phone. There are no hidden taxes or fees.

Hot Desking

Hot Desking Enables any end-user to log into a shared desk phones as if it were their own.

Music on Hold

Music on Hold plays recorded music or marketing messages while your callers are on hold to give your business a professional edge.

Number Porting

Want to keep your existing number or purchase a vanity number? We can move it to our system at no charge.

Unlimited Calling

Call anywhere in the US for as long as you want at no additional charge.

Virtual Fax

Virtual Fax is a personal online faxing service that allows you to send and receive online faxes from any computer.

Voicemail

Setup a voicemail box globally and or per user

Voicemail-To-Email

Voicemail-to-emial is a great way to save time and be more responsive to your clients. Your voicemails are delivered right to your email box as audio files so you can listen to them right away.
